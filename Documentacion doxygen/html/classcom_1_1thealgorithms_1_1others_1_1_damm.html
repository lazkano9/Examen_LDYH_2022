Detailed Description

Damm algorithm is a check digit algorithm that detects all single-digit errors and all adjacent transposition errors. It was presented by H. Michael Damm in 2004. Essential part of the algorithm is a quasigroup of order 10 (i.e. having a 10 Ã— 10 Latin square as the body of its operation table) with the special feature of being weakly totally anti-symmetric. Damm revealed several methods to create totally anti-symmetric quasigroups of order 10 and gave some examples in his doctoral dissertation.
